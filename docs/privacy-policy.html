概述

欢迎使用 AIShotBall（以下简称“本应用”）。我们非常重视您的个人信息与隐私保护。本隐私政策用于说明您在使用本应用过程中，我们会如何收集、使用、存储、共享和保护您的信息，以及您可以如何管理这些信息。 在使用本应用前，请您认真阅读并理解本政策；当您开始使用本应用时，即表示您已知悉并同意本政策中与您相关的内容。

我们收集的信息类型

1. 账户与身份信息 当您使用 Apple 登录时，我们可能收集： • 账户标识符（如 Apple 用户标识） • 昵称、姓名 • 电子邮箱（仅在 Apple 首次授权并提供时） • 登录方式、首次登录时间、最近登录时间 2. 训练与使用数据 当您使用记录、分析、统计、排行榜等功能时，我们可能收集： • 投篮记录与统计数据，如命中/未中、投篮类型、命中率、总出手数等 • 球员名称或您自行设置的昵称 • 您的应用设置与偏好 • 会员订阅状态、到期时间、恢复购买结果 3. 帮助与反馈信息 当您使用“帮助与反馈”功能时，我们可能收集： • 您主动填写的反馈内容 • 您主动填写的联系方式，例如邮箱或手机号 • 您选择附带的应用日志 4. 设备与日志信息 为保障服务可用性和问题排查，我们可能收集： • 设备标识信息，例如设备生成或读取的设备识别标识 • 应用版本、设备型号、操作系统版本 • 与反馈提交、排行榜、统计同步相关的必要请求参数 5. 媒体与相册相关信息 • 您导入用于分析的视频、导出的视频片段或截图，主要保存在您的设备本地 • 为实现导入/保存功能，本应用会在获得您授权后访问系统相册中的视频资源 6. 地区信息 • 您主动填写或在使用相关功能时提交的省、市、区信息，用于排行榜地区分类与展示

信息使用方式

我们收集和使用上述信息，主要用于： • 提供登录、身份识别、账号状态维护等基础功能 • 提供投篮分析、动作分析、数据统计、排行榜、视频导入导出等核心功能 • 保存您的设置偏好与本地使用记录，提升连续使用体验 • 处理会员订阅、恢复购买与权益校验 • 响应您的帮助与反馈请求，排查故障、改进产品体验 • 进行必要的安全保障、异常检测与服务维护 我们不会将您的个人信息出售给第三方，也不会将您的信息用于跨应用追踪或广告定向投放。

权限说明

为实现相关功能，本应用可能会申请以下系统权限： • 相册读取权限：用于导入本地视频进行分析 • 相册写入权限：用于保存导出的视频或图片 • 相机权限：用于拍摄或采集训练视频（如您使用相关功能） 您可以拒绝或在系统设置中关闭相关权限，但这可能导致对应功能无法正常使用。未经您的授权，我们不会访问不必要的权限。

数据存储、传输与安全

• 您的登录凭证、账户标识等敏感信息会优先存储在设备钥匙串等较安全的本地存储区域 • 视频片段、导出内容、部分分析结果与缓存主要保存在您的设备本地 • 投篮统计、排行榜相关资料、反馈内容、联系方式、设备标识及您主动提交的相关信息，可能上传至我们的云端服务，用于实现排行榜、数据同步、反馈处理等功能 • 网络传输过程中，我们会采取合理措施保护数据安全；但互联网传输无法保证绝对安全，请您理解 • 我们会采取访问控制、最小必要、权限隔离等合理安全措施保护您的信息

第三方服务说明

为实现部分功能，本应用可能接入以下第三方服务或平台能力： • Apple 登录：用于账号登录与身份识别 • 微信云开发/腾讯云相关服务：用于云函数调用、排行榜、统计数据处理与反馈提交 • Apple StoreKit：用于处理订阅购买、恢复购买和会员状态校验 上述第三方服务可能基于其自身隐私政策处理必要信息。我们建议您同时查阅相应第三方服务的隐私政策。

数据共享与披露

除以下情形外，我们不会向第三方共享、转让或公开披露您的个人信息： • 为实现本应用核心功能，在必要范围内向第三方技术服务提供方提供 • 根据法律法规、监管要求、司法或行政机关的合法要求进行提供 • 为保护您、本应用、其他用户或公众的人身财产安全所必需 如果发生公司合并、分立、资产转让等情形导致信息转移，我们会依法要求接收方继续履行相应的隐私保护义务。

数据保留

• 本地缓存、视频片段、导出内容等信息，通常保留在您的设备中，直到您主动删除、清理缓存或卸载应用 • 您上传的投篮统计、排行榜资料、反馈内容等信息，将在实现相关业务目的所需期限内保留，法律法规另有要求的除外 • 当超出保留期限或您提出删除请求后，我们会在合理期限内删除或匿名化处理相关信息

您对个人信息的管理权利

您通常可以通过以下方式管理您的信息： 1. 修改昵称、地区信息或相关设置 2. 关闭相册、相机等系统权限 3. 退出登录，清除当前设备上的账户信息 4. 在“我的”页面使用“删除账号”功能，发起删除当前账号及关联云端数据 5. 删除应用，以清除大部分本地存储内容 6. 在“使用 Apple 登录”的系统设置中停止向本应用提供 Apple 账号登录 7. 通过应用内“帮助与反馈”联系我们，申请查询、更正、删除相关云端数据 我们会在核实您的身份后，于合理期限内处理您的请求；但在法律法规要求保留相关信息的情况下，我们可能无法立即删除全部数据。

未成年人保护

如果您是未满 14 周岁的未成年人，请在监护人陪同下阅读并决定是否同意本政策，并在监护人同意后使用本应用。监护人应指导未成年人正确使用本应用并保护其个人信息。

政策更新

我们可能根据产品功能、法律法规或业务变化适时更新本隐私政策。更新后的政策会在应用内公布，并自公布或标注的更新日期起生效。若更新涉及重大变更，我们会以适当方式向您提示。

联系我们

如果您对本隐私政策、个人信息保护或数据删除申请有任何疑问，请通过应用内“帮助与反馈”功能联系我们。

Overview

Welcome to AIShotBall ("the App"). We take your personal information and privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, share, and protect your information when you use the App, and how you can manage that information. Please read this policy carefully before using the App. By using the App, you acknowledge and agree to the terms that apply to you.

Information we collect

1. Account and identity information When you use Sign in with Apple, we may collect: • Account identifiers such as your Apple user identifier • Nickname or name • Email address, only when Apple provides it during initial authorization • Sign-in method, first sign-in time, and recent sign-in time 2. Training and usage data When you use recording, analysis, statistics, and leaderboard features, we may collect: • Shot records and statistical data such as makes, misses, shot type, accuracy, and total attempts • Player names or nicknames you create • App settings and preferences • Membership status, expiration date, and restore-purchase results 3. Help and feedback information When you use Help & Feedback, we may collect: • Feedback content you submit • Contact details you provide, such as email or phone number • App logs you choose to include 4. Device and log information To maintain service availability and investigate issues, we may collect: • Device identifiers created or read on the device • App version, device model, and OS version • Necessary request parameters related to feedback, leaderboards, and stat sync 5. Media and Photos information • Videos, exported clips, and screenshots you import for analysis are mainly stored locally on your device • To support import and save features, the App may access video resources in Photos after you grant permission 6. Region information • Province, city, and district information you submit for regional leaderboard grouping and display

How we use information

We use the information above primarily to: • Provide core account features such as sign-in, identity recognition, and account status maintenance • Deliver key features such as shot analysis, motion analysis, stats, leaderboards, and video import/export • Save your preferences and local usage records to improve continuity • Process memberships, purchase restoration, and entitlement validation • Respond to help and feedback requests, troubleshoot issues, and improve the product • Support security protections, anomaly detection, and service maintenance We do not sell your personal information to third parties, nor do we use it for cross-app tracking or targeted advertising.

Permissions

The App may request the following system permissions: • Photos read access, to import local videos for analysis • Photos write access, to save exported videos or images • Camera access, to record training videos when you use those features You may deny or later disable these permissions in system settings, but some features may stop working properly. We do not access unnecessary permissions without your authorization.

Storage, transfer, and security

• Sensitive information such as login credentials and account identifiers is stored locally in more secure areas such as the Keychain when possible • Video clips, exported content, some analysis results, and caches are mainly stored locally on your device • Shot statistics, leaderboard-related information, feedback, contact details, device identifiers, and other information you submit may be uploaded to our cloud services to support leaderboards, sync, and feedback handling • We take reasonable measures to protect data during transmission, but no internet transmission can be guaranteed absolutely secure • We use reasonable safeguards such as access control, least-privilege design, and permission isolation to protect your information

Third-party services

To provide some features, the App may rely on the following third-party services or platform capabilities: • Sign in with Apple, for account login and identity verification • WeChat Cloud / Tencent Cloud related services, for cloud functions, leaderboards, stat processing, and feedback submission • Apple StoreKit, for subscription purchases, restore purchases, and membership validation These third-party services may process necessary information under their own privacy policies. We recommend reviewing those privacy policies as well.

Sharing and disclosure

We do not share, transfer, or publicly disclose your personal information except in the following cases: • When reasonably necessary to provide the App's core functions through technical service providers • When required by law, regulation, regulators, courts, or administrative authorities • When necessary to protect your safety, the App, other users, or the public If a merger, split, or asset transfer causes information to be transferred, we will require the receiving party to continue honoring applicable privacy obligations where required by law.

Data retention

• Local cache, video clips, and exported content usually remain on your device until you delete them, clear cache, or uninstall the App • Uploaded shot statistics, leaderboard data, and feedback are retained for as long as needed to fulfill the relevant business purpose unless a longer period is required by law • After the retention period expires or after you submit a valid deletion request, we will delete or anonymize relevant information within a reasonable time

Your rights and controls

You can generally manage your information in the following ways: 1. Update your nickname, region information, or related settings 2. Disable system permissions such as Photos or Camera 3. Sign out and clear account information from the current device 4. Use Delete Account on the Profile page to request deletion of the current account and related cloud data 5. Delete the App to remove most local stored content 6. Stop using Sign in with Apple for the App in Apple's system settings 7. Contact us through in-app Help & Feedback to request access, correction, or deletion of related cloud data We will process requests within a reasonable time after verifying your identity, though we may be unable to delete all information immediately when retention is required by law.

Children's privacy

If you are under 14 years old, please review this policy with a parent or legal guardian and use the App only with their consent. Guardians should guide minors in proper use of the App and protection of personal information.

Policy updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time based on product features, legal requirements, or business changes. Updated versions will be published in the App and take effect on the stated effective date. We will provide appropriate notice for material changes.

Contact us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, personal information protection, or data deletion requests, contact us through the in-app Help & Feedback feature.
